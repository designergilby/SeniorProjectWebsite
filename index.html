ENVIRONMENTAL WELLNESS

Environmental wellness inspires us to live a lifestyle that is respectful of our surroundings. This dimension encourages us to live in harmony with planet earth by taking action to protect and preserve it. To bes t execute this in design, the designs should be inhabitable, resilient, spatial, and accessible.
